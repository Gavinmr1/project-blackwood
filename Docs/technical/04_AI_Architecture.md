# AI Architecture

## Overview

The AI system controls all non-player characters and creatures within Project Blackwood.

AI should prioritize believable behavior and create emergent moments.

The goal is not for AI to simply challenge players.

The goal is to make the park feel alive.

---

# AI Categories

The park contains several types of AI.

## Wildlife AI

Controls animals and natural ecosystem behavior.

Examples:
- Deer
- Bears
- Birds
- Predators
- Future creatures

Wildlife should behave independently from the player.

---

# Wildlife Behavior

Animals should:
- Search for food
- Avoid threats
- Protect territory
- Move between areas
- React to weather
- React to player actions

---

# Wildlife States

Example:

Deer:

## Calm

Normal movement.

↓

## Alert

Notices unusual activity.

↓

## Fleeing

Escapes danger.

↓

## Defensive

Protects itself or young.

---

# Visitor AI

Controls campers and park guests.

Visitors should:
- Explore
- Follow trails
- Camp
- Ask for help
- React to danger

---

# Visitor Problems

Visitors create gameplay situations.

Examples:
- Lost hikers
- Injured campers
- Ignored warnings
- Wrong turns
- Emergency situations

---

# Ranger/NPC AI

Future AI support:
- Park staff
- Emergency responders
- Researchers

---

# Cryptid AI

Cryptids are not traditional enemies.

They are intelligent or mysterious entities.

They have:
- Territory
- Motivation
- Patterns
- Behaviors

---

# Cryptid Behavior Examples

## Bigfoot

Behavior:
- Avoids humans
- Observes players
- Protects territory
- Leaves evidence

Possible encounters:
- Tracks
- Sounds
- Brief sightings
- Rare interactions

---

## Unknown Creatures

Behavior:
- React to environment
- Interact with wildlife
- Change based on player actions

---

# AI Decision Making

AI decisions consider:
- Location
- Time
- Weather
- Player actions
- World condition
- Previous events

---

# AI Communication

AI can create indirect signals.

Examples:

Animals:
- Running away
- Warning sounds
- Missing from areas

Visitors:
- Reports
- Rumors
- Sightings

Cryptids:
- Tracks
- Sounds
- Environmental changes

---

# AI Goals

AI should create:
- Discovery
- Tension
- Curiosity
- Stories

Not constant combat.

---

# Design Rule

The best AI moments happen when players are unsure what they saw.
