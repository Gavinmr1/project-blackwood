# World Design

## Overview

Project Blackwood takes place in a large wilderness park where players work as park rangers.

The world should feel peaceful, beautiful, and alive while hiding unexplained mysteries.

The park is designed around exploration, discovery, and emergent events.

---

# First Region: Blackwood Valley

## Overview

Blackwood Valley is the starting area of the game.

It contains:
- Ranger Station
- Forest trails
- Campgrounds
- Lake
- Wildlife areas
- Abandoned locations
- Hidden mysteries

The region should feel large enough to explore but small enough that players learn its landmarks.

---

# Environmental Goals

The park should feel:
- Natural
- Isolated
- Beautiful
- Slightly unsettling

The player should become familiar with the environment before strange events occur.

---

# Major Locations

## Ranger Station

The player's home base.

Purpose:
- Upgrades
- Planning
- Story
- Equipment

---

## Pine Ridge Trail

A beginner hiking trail.

Contains:
- Trail signs
- Small bridges
- Scenic overlooks
- Wildlife encounters

Primary area for learning ranger tasks.

---

## Blackwood Lake

Large body of water.

Contains:
- Fishing area
- Boat dock
- Campground
- Wildlife habitat

Potential events:
- Missing campers
- Strange lights
- Unusual animal behavior

---

## Whispering Woods

Dense forest area.

Contains:
- Old trees
- Hidden paths
- Ranger journal locations
- Unknown activity

This is the first area where the park feels different.

---

## Old Ranger Cabin

An abandoned structure.

Purpose:
- Story discovery
- Previous ranger clues
- Hidden equipment

---

## Fire Lookout Tower

High elevation location.

Purpose:
- Fire monitoring
- Weather observation
- Emergency communication

Potential mystery location.

---

# Traversal

Players move through the park using:
- Walking
- Ranger vehicle
- Future vehicles:
  - Boats
  - ATVs
  - Snowmobiles
  - Helicopters

---

# Environmental Systems

The park includes:

## Weather

Examples:
- Rain
- Storms
- Fog
- Snow
- Heat waves

Weather affects gameplay.

---

## Time

Day/night cycle affects:
- Wildlife
- Visibility
- Events
- Player safety

---

## Seasons

Future expansion:
- Spring
- Summer
- Fall
- Winter

---

# Hidden Areas

The park contains locations players discover over time.

Examples:
- Caves
- Abandoned structures
- Old trails
- Underground areas
- Unknown locations

---

# Design Rule

Players should learn the park before the park changes.

The familiar should become unfamiliar.
