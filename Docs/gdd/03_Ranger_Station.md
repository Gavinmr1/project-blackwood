# Ranger Station

## Overview

The ranger station is the player's home base and the center of all park operations.

It serves as:
- A safe zone
- Upgrade hub
- Planning area
- Story location
- Multiplayer gathering space

The station should visibly evolve as the players improve the park.

---

# Starting Station

Players begin with a small ranger outpost.

Features:
- Small cabin
- Basic radio
- Storage locker
- Work desk
- Generator
- Parking area
- Single ranger vehicle

The station should feel isolated but functional.

---

# Station Activities

Before leaving the station players can:
- Review assignments
- Check weather
- Read ranger reports
- Manage equipment
- Purchase upgrades
- View park map
- Review discovered evidence

---

# Station Upgrades

The station grows through player progress.

## Phase 1: Basic Outpost

Starting equipment:
- Flashlights
- Basic tools
- First aid supplies
- Ranger truck
- Radio

---

## Phase 2: Improved Station

Unlocks:
- Larger storage
- Better equipment
- Workshop
- Vehicle upgrades
- Additional rooms

---

## Phase 3: Ranger Headquarters

Unlocks:
- Multiple vehicles
- Research equipment
- Wildlife facilities
- Emergency response tools
- Expanded communications

---

## Phase 4: Advanced Operations Center

Unlocks:
- Helicopter pad
- Weather monitoring
- Search and rescue equipment
- Park surveillance
- Advanced investigation tools

---

# Station Personalization

Players can customize the station.

Examples:
- Decorations
- Trophy items
- Found objects
- Photos
- Maps
- Journal pages
- Cryptid evidence

The station becomes a record of the team's journey.

---

# Multiplayer Station Design

The station should support cooperation.

Players can:
- Prepare together
- Review maps
- Discuss plans
- Share equipment
- Display discoveries

The station should feel like a shared home.

---

# Story Integration

The previous ranger's presence should be discovered through the station.

Possible discoveries:
- Hidden journals
- Old equipment
- Unfinished investigations
- Secret rooms
- Archived reports

The station slowly reveals the history of the park.

---

# Design Rule

The station should never feel like a menu.

It should feel like a place players return to.
