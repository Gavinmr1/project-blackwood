# Gameplay Loop

## Overview

Project Blackwood is built around the daily life of a park ranger.

Players begin each day at their ranger station, receive assignments, prepare equipment, explore the park, complete ranger duties, respond to emergencies, and return to improve their station.

The core loop should be satisfying even without supernatural events.

---

# Core Daily Loop

## 1. Start Day

Players begin at the ranger station.

Activities:
- Review assignments
- Check weather
- Review park alerts
- Choose equipment
- Select vehicle
- Plan route

The station acts as the player's home base.

---

# 2. Patrol The Park

Players leave the station and enter the wilderness.

Activities:
- Drive ranger vehicles
- Hike trails
- Monitor wildlife
- Inspect facilities
- Help visitors
- Search for problems

The park should feel alive and constantly changing.

---

# 3. Complete Ranger Tasks

Tasks are the main source of progression.

Examples:

## Maintenance
- Repair damaged signs
- Replace trail markers
- Fix bridges
- Clear fallen trees
- Repair cabins

## Environmental
- Remove trash
- Maintain campsites
- Restore habitats
- Monitor ecosystems

## Emergency
- Fight fires
- Rescue hikers
- Respond to accidents
- Assist lost visitors

---

# 4. Unexpected Events

While working, players may encounter unexpected situations.

Examples:
- Wildlife encounters
- Severe weather
- Missing hikers
- Equipment failures
- Strange discoveries
- Supernatural events

Events should emerge naturally.

---

# 5. Return To Station

Players return with completed work.

Rewards:
- Money
- Reputation
- Park improvement
- New equipment
- New areas

---

# 6. Upgrade And Prepare

The ranger station evolves over time.

Players can improve:
- Equipment
- Vehicles
- Facilities
- Tools
- Research capabilities

---

# Player Session Goals

A successful session should provide:
- A sense of progress
- A memorable story
- Improved park condition
- New discoveries

---

# Design Rule

The player should always feel like a park ranger first.

Supernatural events enhance the experience but should never replace the core ranger fantasy.
