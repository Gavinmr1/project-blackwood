# Progression System

## Overview

Progression in Project Blackwood represents the player's growth from a new park ranger into an experienced wilderness operator.

Players improve through:
- Equipment upgrades
- Ranger station improvements
- Park restoration
- Knowledge
- Reputation

Progression should feel like becoming a better ranger.

---

# Player Progression

Players advance through experience gained from:
- Completing ranger tasks
- Helping visitors
- Responding to emergencies
- Discovering mysteries
- Improving the park

---

# Ranger Rank

Players progress through ranger ranks.

Example:

## New Recruit

Starting rank.

Unlocked:
- Basic tools
- Ranger truck
- Starter equipment

---

## Field Ranger

Unlocked:
- Better equipment
- Wildlife tools
- Improved vehicles

---

## Senior Ranger

Unlocked:
- Advanced rescue equipment
- Leadership abilities
- Larger assignments

---

## Park Supervisor

Unlocked:
- Advanced station upgrades
- Specialized equipment
- Park management systems

---

# Equipment Progression

Equipment improves through upgrades.

---

## Starting Equipment

Players begin with:
- Flashlight
- Radio
- First aid kit
- Basic tools
- Map

---

## Ranger Tools

Future upgrades:

### Flashlights

Progression:
Basic flashlight
↓
High-powered flashlight
↓
Thermal/Night vision equipment

---

### Communication

Progression:
Basic radio
↓
Long range radio
↓
Advanced communication network

---

### Rescue Equipment

Progression:
Basic first aid
↓
Rescue kits
↓
Advanced emergency equipment

---

### Investigation Equipment

Progression:
Notebook
↓
Camera
↓
Sensors
↓
Research equipment

---

# Vehicle Progression

Vehicles expand player capability.

---

## Ranger Truck

Starting vehicle.

Used for:
- Transportation
- Equipment storage
- Basic patrols

---

## ATV

Unlocked later.

Used for:
- Trails
- Rough terrain
- Faster response

---

## Boat

Used for:
- Lakes
- Water rescues
- Wildlife observation

---

## Helicopter

Late-game unlock.

Used for:
- Large emergencies
- Rapid travel
- Search missions

---

# Ranger Station Progression

The station evolves alongside the player.

Upgrades unlock:
- Storage
- Workshops
- Medical facilities
- Research rooms
- Vehicle areas
- Communication systems

---

# Park Reputation

The player's actions affect park reputation.

Higher reputation leads to:
- More visitors
- Funding
- New opportunities
- Expanded regions

---

# Knowledge Progression

Some upgrades are not purchased.

Players learn through experience.

Examples:
- Wildlife behavior
- Park shortcuts
- Strange events
- Hidden locations
- Ranger journals

Knowledge becomes a permanent advantage.

---

# Design Rule

Progression should make players feel:
"I understand this park better."

Not just:
"My stats are higher."
